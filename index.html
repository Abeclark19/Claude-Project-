On a Mission Against Freelancer Burnout

Being self-employed is first and foremost a lifestyle choice. You leave employment to find space, organizational freedom, and more time for yourself.

— the time each project actually takes,
— the time needed to grow and run their business,

A freelancer who undercharges slowly but surely builds their own fragility — financial and mental.
